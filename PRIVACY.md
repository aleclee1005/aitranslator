# Privacy Policy for Interpreter

**Last updated: May 14, 2026**

## Overview

Interpreter ("the App") is a real-time speech translation app for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- All speech recognition is performed on-device using Apple's Speech framework.
- Translation via Apple Translation is performed entirely on-device; no audio or text leaves your device.
- When using the optional Groq cloud translation engine, only the recognized text (not audio) is sent to Groq's API for translation. No user identifiers are attached.

## Microphone and Speech Recognition

The App requests access to your microphone and speech recognition solely to convert spoken words into text for translation. Audio is processed locally on your device and is never stored or uploaded by the App.

## Translation History

Session history (original text and translations) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time by removing the App.

## Third-Party Services

When the Groq engine is selected in Settings, recognized text is sent to [Groq, Inc.](https://groq.com) for translation. Please review [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/) for details on how they handle data. No other third-party services are used.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
